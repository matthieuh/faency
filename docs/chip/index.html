Chip

Useful to emphasise information to the user, works best with single word values.

At Containous, our goal is to make networking boring.
<Chip variant="green">At Containous, our goal is to make networking boring.</Chip>

Props

PropTypeDescription
childrenReactNodeThe content to render inside the Chip
variantgray | blue | lightBlue | purple | green | orange | redThe variant to apply
size0 | 1 | 2The size to apply
truncateBooleanWhether it should truncate or not

Text truncation

When true this will truncate your text based on its maximum width.

At Containous, our goal is to make networking boring.
<Flex mb="2" maxWidth="150px">
  <Chip truncate title="At Containous, our goal is to make networking boring.">
    At Containous, our goal is to make networking boring.
  </Chip>
</Flex>
